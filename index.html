About Us


Learning For Life.

 The School is a Co-educational English medium school established by P.Arukutty Gounder Memorial Trust. It is a charitable, service oriented and non profit making body. The aim of the trust is to provide Integral Education with a keen sense of discipline, responsibility and initiative to Self reliance, Integrity and loyalty. The individual child is the core of whole program. Vision
Our vision is to develop young men with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others, and the courage to act on their beliefs. We stress the spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional and physical development of each child
